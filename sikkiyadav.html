सिक्का यादव | Sikki Yadav

Vice District Head, Chandrapur

"The foundation tested by the storm stands the strongest."

5x Consecutive Corporator
15Y Municipality Chairman

Sikki Yadav (Vinod Kumar Ramu Yadav) is a symbol of public trust who transformed intense personal struggle into a prolific political career. After facing a criminal conspiracy intended to silence his stance against crime, he emerged with 'Truth and Courage' as his shield.

सिक्का यादव (विनोद कुमार रामू यादव) हे जनविश्वासाचे प्रतीक आहेत, ज्यांनी वैयक्तिक संघर्षाला यशस्वी राजकीय कारकिर्दीत बदलले. गुन्हेगारीविरुद्ध आवाज उठवल्यामुळे झालेल्या षडयंत्राला त्यांनी 'सत्य आणि संयम' या बळावर हरवले.

प्रवास संघर्षाचा | The Timeline of Truth

1995

The Foundation

Earned B.Sc degree from Guru Nanak College of Science. Began career at Shivalaya Housing Finance, advocating for the needy.

1996

Political Debut

Overcame false legal conspiracies. With the support of Ajay Swami, contested and won his first local ward election, starting his rehabilitation through service.

Today

Legacy of Loyalty

25 years of continuous service to Shiv Sena. Held offices as Vice President of Municipality and Senate Member of Nagpur University.
